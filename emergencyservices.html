    Emergency Services

    Edmonton is a huge city and finding services can be difficult if not impossible at times. You can find below links and directions to edmontons emergency services!!!

    Hospitals


    While hospitals have a long waiting time, they are also filled with skilled medical professionals that can quickly help you when your turn comes. They are several hospitals located throughout the city, and wait times vary from one hour to 6 hours.


    Medical Clinics


    If your emergency isn't too severe, a medical clinic is the place to go! Ranging from a common cold to stitches, a doctor will help you out with a smaller wait time than a hospital.


    Fire Stations


    Cat stuck in a tree? House on fire? Car accident? Don't fret, the firefighters are here! Always the first to arrive, these friendly folks will help out no matter how small the emergency! Call 911 for help.


    Police Stations


    Police stations are located throughout the city in various communities. Call 911 for help.
